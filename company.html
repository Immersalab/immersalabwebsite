Our Mission

3D visualization should be as easy as writing code. For too long, professional 3D rendering has been locked behind expensive software, powerful hardware, and steep learning curves.

We're changing that. Glint3D brings browser-native rendering, automation-first design, and AI-ready architecture to everyone. Whether you're a solo developer, a Fortune 500 company, or an AI researcher, beautiful 3D should be accessible with just a few lines of code.

Open source. Browser-native. AI-ready. This is 3D for the modern web.

Democratizing 3D

Making professional visualization accessible to developers, designers, and AI systems worldwide.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we build

Open by Default

We believe powerful tools should be accessible to everyone. Open source isn't just our license—it's our philosophy.

AI-First Design

Built for humans, designed for automation. Every API, every workflow is crafted with AI agents and automation in mind.

Web-Native

The browser is the most democratic platform ever created. We're building for the web because it reaches everyone.

Community Driven

Our best ideas come from our users. We build with our community, not just for them.

Performance Matters

Beautiful shouldn't mean slow. We obsess over performance so creativity flows without friction.

Always Learning

Technology evolves fast. We stay curious, embrace new ideas, and aren't afraid to be wrong if it means moving forward.
