Product Visualization Packs

Transform SKU catalogs overnight. Batch-render hero shots, 360° turntables, and color variants with consistent lighting and perfect framing.

{
  "scene": "product_hero_shot",
  "models": ["SKU_001.glb", "SKU_002.glb"],
  "variants": ["red", "blue", "black"],
  "outputs": ["hero", "turntable", "lifestyle"]
}

Synthetic Data for AI/Robotics

Generate deterministic training datasets. Perfect labels, controlled domain randomization, and exact reproducibility for faster ML development.

$ python generate_dataset.py
generator = DatasetGenerator(
  scene_template='warehouse.json',
  randomization_config=random_config
)
dataset = generator.generate(count=10000)

Financial Visualization & Storytelling

From CSV to cinematic. Programmatically generate animated portfolio drawdowns, risk scenarios, and branded report presentations.

                 --template quarterly_report.json
