Example Platforms Built on Glint3D

From product visualization to synthetic data and beyond — one renderer, endless possibilities

VizPack

Product Visualization Platform. Automated e-commerce visuals. Bulk-generate hero shots, 360° turntables, and color variants overnight. Transform SKU catalogs without photo shoots.

DataGen

Synthetic Training Data Platform. Generate labeled datasets at scale for AI/robotics. Deterministic scenes with perfect annotations, controlled randomization, and exact reproducibility.

FinViz

Financial Storytelling Platform. Transform CSV data into cinematic dashboards. Automated portfolio reports, risk visualizations, and branded presentations that update with live data.

EduViz

STEM Learning Platform. Interactive 3D visualizations for education. Physics simulations, molecular structures, architectural walkthroughs. Share with a link, no software required.

GameViz

Game Development Platform. Scene testing and asset preview pipelines. Batch-render lighting setups, test camera angles, validate asset quality before engine import.

MedViz

Medical Training Platform. Anatomical visualizations and surgical training datasets. Generate procedural variations for medical education and AI-assisted diagnosis systems.

ArchViz

Architecture Platform. Automated building renders and virtual tours. Generate client presentations with different materials, lighting conditions, and seasonal variations.

CinemaViz

Previs & VFX Platform. Pre-visualization for film and animation. Rapid scene blocking, camera planning, and lighting tests. Export to production pipelines.
